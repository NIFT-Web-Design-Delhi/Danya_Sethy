ABOUT

The Blues of the Pink is a craft cluster initiative undertaken by students of the National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT), New Delhi. We learned more about the ancient craft of blue pottery throughout our journey and realized how important it is to keep these crafts alive.
We were able to get a deep insight into the know-how of blue pottery which led us to our project. It aims to bring awareness amongst the younger generation through a method of fun and games.

About The Artisian

Sanjay Prajapat ji is a blue pottery artisan from Jaipur and has been practicing this craft since he was 10 years old. The craft of blue pottery has been in his family for several generations and he learnt it from his father. He loves making motifs related to gods and goddesses, hunting scenes and mosaics. He strongly believes in gender equality and has taught his wife the craft too.
