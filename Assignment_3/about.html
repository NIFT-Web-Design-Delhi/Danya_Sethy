The Blues of the Pink

ABOUT

The Blues of the Pink is a craft cluster initiative undertaken by students of the National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT), New Delhi. We learned more about the ancient craft of blue pottery throughout our journey and realized how important it is to keep these crafts alive.
We were able to get a deep insight into the know-how of blue pottery which led us to our project. It aims to bring awareness amongst the younger generation through a method of fun and games.

Sanjay Ji's Journey

My family used to work with ivory before blue pottery. We used to work in Maharani Gayatri Devi’s undertaking in secrecy. My grandfather realised that he enjoyed the craft of Blue Pottery more, along with the fact that he knew how to work on a potter’s wheel. This is small scale work that you can even do at home. So, he left Ivory for Blue Pottery. After that, my father took over and now I'm working with Blue Pottery for the past 20 years.
